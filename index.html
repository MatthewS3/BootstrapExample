The Console War of November 2020

The Play Station 5 has been a revolutionary piece of tech in the divided world of consoles. Play station knew they had to up their game as the announcment of the Xbox Series X had just been released and everybody was shocked at the crazy fridge like design. What no body thought was that Play Station was going to absolutely shock the world with the new very large but creative but also innovative design of the Play Station 5.
